How You Can Help

Small actions can save lives. Here is what you can do.

Immediate Actions

Food & Water

Leave some water and food for stray cats and dogs in your neighborhood, especially during summer or winter.

Report & Rescue

If you see an injured animal, contact us or the local animal services immediately. Don't ignore them.

Adopt or Foster

Adopting saves two lives: the one you adopt and the one that takes its place in the shelter.
