A Few Things About Us

We are a group of girls based in Greece with a mission: to help reduce the number of stray animals seeking a home. Every animal deserves a loving family, and we are here to ensure this happens.

Our Goal

We aim to advise and inform as many people as possible about the abandonment of dogs, cats, and other animals. Raising awareness is the only way to find homes for stray pets and minimize the problem in our world.
